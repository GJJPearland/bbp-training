Training Overview

This training covers OSHA's Bloodborne Pathogen Standard as it applies to our BJJ school environment

  • Who: All staff members who may encounter blood during their duties
  • Frequency: Required annually
  • Standard: OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1030
  • Purpose: Protect workers from health hazards related to bloodborne pathogens

What Are Bloodborne Pathogens?

Infectious microorganisms found in human blood that can cause serious diseases

Hepatitis B (HBV)

Attacks the liver

Hepatitis C (HCV)

Attacks the liver

HIV

Attacks the immune system

Transmission: Through contact with infected blood or certain body fluids
